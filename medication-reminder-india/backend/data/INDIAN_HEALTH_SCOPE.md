## Supported Indian Health Conditions

This project focuses on high-impact diseases prevalent in India, selected using public health data and government guidelines.

### Chronic Conditions
- Type-2 Diabetes
- Hypertension
- Cardiovascular Diseases
- Asthma / COPD

### Acute Conditions
- Common bacterial infections requiring antibiotics

### Rationale
- High prevalence in India (NFHS)
- Included in NLEM
- Strong medication adherence dependency
- Suitable for AI-based risk modeling

This scope ensures clinical relevance while maintaining data privacy.
